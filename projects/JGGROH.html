Just Gotta Get Right Outta Here

Project link

This game is a school project originally made using Android Studio during my last year of Master (2020). The teacher thought it had potential and asked our team to port it under Unity 2019.3. Its server part is written in plain C++.

It is an online puzzle game running on Android where up to four players have to collaborate in order to reach the end of the level. Each character has a special ability, such as pushing blocks or breaking walls, and can unlock another one by collecting items.
