Together We Stand

Project link | February 2021

This game was made for the one-week longBrackeys Jam 2021.1, which theme was Stronger Together, using Unity 2020.2.

The only objective is to defeat all the ennemies in the boss room, which can be accessed directly when the game starts. To do so, the player can control different characters with specific characteristics, such as a great move speed, great damage or passive traits such as gaining health when killing an ennemy. At the beginning, only one character is available ; the player has to explore the different regions of the level in order to unlock others, raising its chance to win.


Even though I don't plan to work on the game anymore, I still created its modules in a scalable way so that it could be easily extended and integrated out of the box in my future projects. As any other jam, I tried to focus on specific areas of development in this one. I thus wanted to make use of everything the Cinemachine free-look camera has to offer, as well as the Odin inspector which brings a lot of quality of life improvements to Unity. As always, I wanted the game to be playable no matter what type of controller is used.
Finally, taking place during the Unity's Lunar New Year Mega Bundle, which I bought, it was also a good way to test some of the assets contained in this pack, such as the Little Heroes Mega Pack which brang a great amount of diversity to the game.
