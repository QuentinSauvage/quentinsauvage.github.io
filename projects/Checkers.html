This project was made as a school project during my third year of Bachelor. It is a classic checkers game which uses the regular rules, the main focus of the project being on the artificial intelligence, in order to organize a tournament between every AI made by the students groups, which our AI won.

It was written in plain C, with a network interface given by our teacher. In order to be performant, it implements the Minimax and Alpha-beta algorithms, trying to predict the opponent's moves and to find the best corresponding action. The whole game is played in the console.
