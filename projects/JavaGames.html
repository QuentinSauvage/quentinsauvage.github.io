This collection of games was made using Java and JavaFX during my second year of Bachelor. Each game was a one-week long development and resolves around an OOP concept (polymorphism, design patterns and so on) or more general IT specificities (threading, AI, ...)..
Most of the games featured are remake of the most classic video games and board games such as Mario Bros, Connect Four, Othello, Pong or Tron. Most of them can be played against an AI, but as it was optional, and given the short length of each project, they are for the most part quite basic.
