Fish And Ships

Project link | November 2021

This game was made for the one-week longVideo Games Lab: Game Jam 2021, which theme was Arcade, using Unity 2020.3.

The goal is simply to collect has much fish as possible in limited time. To do so, the player has to land the boat on a planet, collect fish then deposit everything inside a portal. I used vertex coloring to handle the progress of the player when collecting fish. That way, it also gives feedback to the player regarding the state of the planet they are on.
