Tetris

Project link | March 2018

This game was made as a school project during my third year of Bachelor, using Java and JavaFX. It is based on Tetris DS, a game made by Nintendo in 2006.

In this game, two players face each other by placing tiles on their corresponding grid. When they complete a line, the limit between the two grids is pushed towards the opponent, and if it comes too close to the bottom of the grid, the player is defeated. They can store a tile if they want to place it later, and can see the next four tiles they will get. The game is also playable in standard mode, with only one player.
