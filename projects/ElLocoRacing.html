El Loco Racing

Project link | December 2019

This game was made as a school project during my last year of Master, using Unity 2019.2 and its Standard Assets. It is a very classic racing game, where the player can play against up to nine other cars controlled by the computer, featuring two tracks.

It is my first serious experience using Unity ; this way, I got to experiment for the first time with a lot of features such as AI and car engine tweaking, lighting (car headlights, lampposts), fog, LODs, post-processing (such as bloom, depth of field and vignette) and assets integration under Unity. I also used EasyRoads3D to create the tracks, which I found quite difficult as I had to make sure the road was properly set right on the terrain.
