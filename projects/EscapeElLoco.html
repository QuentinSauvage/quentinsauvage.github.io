Escape El Loco

Project link | October 2019

This game was made as a school project during my last year of Master, using C++ and SFML. Its purpose was to familiarise myself with collision handling and the game loop management in order to maintain a correct framerate.

It is a small game where the player controls two characters at the same time, with the same inputs: if one character goes left, the other one also goes left. The goal is to make each character go through a door located at the end of the level, but before that, they have to unlock these doors by collecting a key hidden somewhere in the level.
Each character progresses on their side of the level and can collect coins that will temporarily make blocks appear or disappear on the other side, helping the other character to reach the end of the level.
