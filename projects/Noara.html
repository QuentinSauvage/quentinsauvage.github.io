Noara: The Conspiracy

Steam Page | Unity, C# | March 2021 - December 2025

Noara is a turn-based tactical game with a strong focus on PvP, made with Unity.

I worked on its prototyping and its conception and assured the continuous development of the game by working closely with GD and QA teams and of course the rest of my team. I also handled the core features of the game such as:

  • Integration of new units playable and their skills.
  • Networking with Photon for social interaction, matchmaking, leaderboards and synchronization between players ingame.
  • Custom backend integration with players data (account, friends list, inventory, events, currencies).
  • Steam integration and deployment.
  • Conception and redesign of the game UI.
  • AI behaviours.
  • Optimization.
