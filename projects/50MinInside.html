50 Minutes Inside

Project link

This game was made as a school project during my last year of Master, Unreal Engine 4.24. Its purpose was to have a first experience with the engine, as well as to create an AI based on behaviour trees.

The game is based on Descent, a FPS made in 1995. It features a 6DoF spaceship that needs to destroy every target to complete the level. To do that, it can collect power-ups and strong weapons. I were supposed to get paid assets by our school, but Covid19 hit us, I then ended up with free textures and meshes made by myself using Blender.
