50 Minutes Inside

Project link | February 2020 - March 2020

This game was made as a school project during my last year of Master, using Unreal Engine 4.24. Its main focus was on an Artificial Intelligence based on behaviour trees.

The game is based on Descent, a FPS made in 1995. It features a 6DoF spaceship that needs to destroy every target to complete the level. To do so, it can collect power-ups and strong weapons. I was supposed to get assets from the university, but Covid19 hit us so I worked with free textures and meshes made by myself using Blender. All that significantly affected the development of the project.
